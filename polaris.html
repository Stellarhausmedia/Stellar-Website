What it does

All day, without being asked.

Every practice loses patients in the same five gaps. Polaris sits in each one.

  • ✓Answers the missed call. The phone rings out at lunch — the caller gets a message back within seconds, with a link to book. The patient who would have dialled the next clinic stays yours.
  • ✓Chases confirmations. Booked patients get a friendly reminder chain — days before, the day before, hours before — and a reply confirms them. Your team sees at a glance who's solid and who needs a call.
  • ✓Wins back no-shows. Anyone who misses gets a same-day "life happens — want a new time?" message, then a gentle follow-up. Most rebook themselves.
  • ✓Brings lapsed patients home. Patients overdue for a visit get a polite nudge with a booking link — the quiet revenue sitting in every practice's records.
  • ✓Asks for the review. After a good visit, one honest request for a Google review. No filtering, no gating — just asked at the right moment, every time.
