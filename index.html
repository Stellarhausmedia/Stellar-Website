The questions dentists ask us

Straight answers

How quickly can we launch?

Most systems go live in days, not months.

Will this replace my receptionist?

No. The front desk works alongside your team. It takes the overflow, the after-hours calls and the follow-ups they can't get to.

Can you work with my existing website?

Yes. We can plug into what you already have, or build you something new.

What if I already have an agency?

We can run alongside them, or take over what isn't working. Usually we own the part they don't: the seconds after an enquiry lands, where most clinics lose the patient.

How soon will I see results?

First booked consults usually come within the first few weeks. We won't hand you a made-up number — what we book depends on your treatments, your area and your ad spend, and we'll be straight about that from day one.

How many patients can I expect?

We won't promise a number we can't back yet. We'll give you a realistic range on the call, not a fantasy on a webpage.

Do I own everything you create?

Yes. The content, the site and the assets are yours.

What happens if we stop working together?

You keep everything that's yours, and there's no lock-in. We'd rather you stay because it works.

Do I pay for the ad budget?

Yes, and it goes straight to Meta, under your control. What you pay us is separate — for the patients we book, and any work you've approved.
