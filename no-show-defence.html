Turn booked appointments into kept ones.

Booking a patient is only half the job — too many quietly don't show. No-Show Defence warms every patient up before the day, and automatically wins back anyone who slips through. You pay once it's built and running.

What it is

Two ways we protect your chairs.

The warm-up

Every booked patient gets a personalised "what to expect" pack — a warm confirmation, real before/afters of their exact treatment, and a plain walk-through of the visit. They arrive reassured and ready, not nervous and wavering.

The recovery

If a patient still misses their slot, we reach out within minutes — "sorry we missed you, shall we find another time?" — and offer the next opening. A no-show becomes a rebooking instead of lost revenue.

How it works

Five gentle touches.

An instant, warm confirmation.
